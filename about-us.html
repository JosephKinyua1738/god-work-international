Who We Are

God's Work International is a nonprofit organization dedicated to improving lives through targeted humanitarian initiatives in Kenya, specifically in Murang’a County. Founded in February 2023, we focus on empowering young girls and boys through hygiene and mentorship programs while providing essential care and support to the elderly.

Mission

To empower girls and boys through hygiene education and mentorship while providing essential care and support to the elderly, fostering dignity, health, and hope in underserved communities.

Vision

A world where every young person has access to hygiene resources and mentorship, and where the elderly live with dignity, care, and support.

Our Story

What began as a small initiative in just 5 schools, supporting 100 girls with sanitary pads, has now grown into a movement impacting over 51 schools, 1,040 girls, and 400 boys. We recognized the urgent need for hygiene education and mentorship among school-going children, as well as the neglect faced by many elderly individuals in our communities. Through dedication and collaboration, we have expanded our reach, distributing not only sanitary pads but also tissue paper, socks, underwear, and other essential hygiene products.

More of our story – Transforming Lives Through Love and Action

At God’s Work International, our journey began with a simple but powerful vision: to restore dignity, hope, and opportunity to the most vulnerable in Murang’a County, Kenya. What started as a small act of kindness in February 2023 has blossomed into a movement touching thousands of lives.

How It All Began

We launched with just 5 schools and 100 girls, providing each with two packets of sanitary pads—a basic necessity many could not afford. The need was heartbreaking:

>> Girls missing school for days each month because they had no pads.
>> Boys wearing dirty and worn-out garmets, facing ridicule and infections.
>> Elderly men and women abandoned, struggling to find even one meal a day.

Since our inception, we have grown from supporting 5 schools (100 girls) to 51 schools (1,040 girls and 400 boys). We distribute sanitary pads, tissue paper, socks, underwear, and hygiene education materials to students. For the elderly, we provide food supplies and host annual December events to bring joy and care to their lives.
