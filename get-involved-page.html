Why support our cause?

At Gods Work International, we believe that lasting change begins with small, consistent acts of love and service. Our work goes beyond giving — it’s about restoring dignity, opening doors to opportunity, and walking alongside the most vulnerable with compassion and purpose. Your support enables us to reach girls who would otherwise miss school, boys who need guidance and hygiene essentials, and elderly individuals who deserve care and respect in their later years. Every contribution you make brings hope to a life and strengthens an entire community.

Here’s Why Your Support Matters:

>> Break the Cycle of Poverty – Every donation directly benefits those in need.
>> Break the Cycle of Poverty – We empower communities for long-term transformation.
>> Break the Cycle of Poverty – Educated and healthy children have better futures.
>> Restore Dignity to the Elderly – No one should suffer neglect in old age.
>> End Period Poverty – Keeping girls in school improves gender equality.

Our Community Impact

For over a year, God's Work International has been dedicated to restoring dignity and hope across Murang’a County, Kenya. What began in just 5 schools has now blossomed into a growing movement changing the lives of children and the elderly alike. With the support of our generous partners and volunteers, we’re creating a future where no one is left behind.

Reached 51 schools, up from just 5 in early 2023
Provided 1,040 girls and 400 boys with hygiene products and mentorship
Distributed sanitary pads, underwear, socks, and tissue to students

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about getting involved with our organization.

What do I need to Volunteer?

Our volunteers must be at least 16 years old (or accompanied by an adult), complete our application process, and attend an orientation session. It is open to anyone willing to help

How much of the donations goes directly to the programs?

We're proud that 85% of all donations go directly to our programs and services. The remaining 15% covers essential administrative costs and fundraising efforts that allow us to continue and expand our work.

Can I volunteer as team or group?

Absolutely! We welcome group volunteering from companies, schools, religious organizations, and other community groups. We can accommodate groups of various sizes and can work with you to find a project that matches your interests and availability.

How can my company be involved?

Companies can partner with us through corporate donations, matching gift programs, sponsorships, and employee volunteer opportunities. We also offer custom corporate social responsibility programs tailored to your company's goals and interests.

Can I specify where I want my donation to go?

Yes, you can designate your donation for a specific program or initiative. During the donation process, you'll have the option to select which area you'd like your contribution to support.
